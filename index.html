UON Academic Standards

  • Minimum CGPA of 2.00 required for degree completion
  • CGPA of 3.50 or above qualifies for Dean's List
  • CGPA of 3.75 or above earns High Honors recognition
  • Students with CGPA below 2.00 are placed on academic probation
  • Consistent performance above 3.00 demonstrates strong academic standing
